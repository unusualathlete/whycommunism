The State Returns to Society

On 28 March 1871, Paris did something more difficult than changing its rulers. It began changing the relation between rulers and ruled.

The Commune made public offices elective and recallable. It cut official salaries to an ordinary worker’s level. It replaced the standing army with the armed population. Its delegates worked under public responsibility instead of receiving sovereignty for several years and then disappearing behind the state.

None of these measures completed communism. Paris remained surrounded, hungry, divided, and at war. The Commune lasted only seventy-two days.

Its importance lies in the movement it began. Powers made by society, but raised above society, were being taken back.

Marx called this the reabsorption of state power by society. The phrase gives a more exact picture than either “take the state” or “abolish the state.” Working people did not need to destroy every record, school, sewer, railway schedule, workshop, or means of common defense. They needed to break the social relation in which these common capacities returned as an authority over them.

Keep the useful function. End its alienated rule.

Before the Commune, a necessary distinction

The modern state concentrates real social capacities:

  • it records births, land, disease, trade, and infrastructure;
  • it organizes roads, water, communication, and disaster response;
  • it trains specialists;
  • it gathers taxes and allocates resources;
  • it makes and enforces binding decisions; and
  • it commands police, prisons, courts, and armed forces.

These capacities are not all the same.

A water laboratory and a political police unit are both state institutions, but their social content differs. Clean water remains necessary after class rule. A force protecting private property against dispossessed people does not. A public health record may need protection and common use. A secret file used to control dissidents should not survive because its paper is administratively useful.

The problem cannot be solved by dividing the state into a good half and a bad half. The same institution can mix social knowledge with class command. A transport ministry may hold indispensable technical information while organizing routes around property values, segregated districts, and profitable commerce. A court may settle real conflicts while protecting the landlord’s claim over the tenant’s home.

Reabsorption therefore has three movements:

  1. Break the machinery through which one class rules another.
  2. Preserve and transform functions that common life still needs.
  3. Spread the knowledge and authority required to perform those functions through society.

This is neither administrative continuity nor administrative amnesia.

What Marx saw in Paris

Marx’s account of the Commune developed while the struggle was still alive. His drafts are especially revealing because they show him searching for the right concept.

The Commune was not a parliamentary body that issued wishes to an executive power. It was a working body that joined decision and execution. Officials became responsible servants. The police lost its political character and answered to the Commune. Public office ceased to be the private property of a trained caste. The standing army, the physical center of a power above society, was replaced by the armed people.

Marx did not praise these forms because they were small or informal. Paris was a city of nearly two million people. The problem was political responsibility.

A delegate can carry a decision across a large territory without becoming sovereign over the people who sent the delegate. A specialist can possess knowledge without possessing the institution. A coordinating body can make a binding decision without turning every participating commune into a subordinate office.

The Commune suggested a republic built upward through connected self-government:

Text alternative: Workplaces, neighborhoods, and communes send mandated and recallable delegates into regional and wider coordination. Decisions, resources, and public reasons return to the bodies that must carry them out. Experience and correction travel upward again, and delegates can be replaced.

The diagram shows circulation, not a ladder of worth. Wider scale gives a body a wider task. It does not make the people inside it a higher kind of person.

From Commune to Commonalty

In 1875, Engels criticized the phrase “free people’s state.” A state standing free in relation to its citizens is precisely the problem. He proposed the German word Gemeinwesen instead.

The word can mean community, common being, or commonalty. Commonalty is useful because it names an organized common life without smuggling the old state relation back into the idea.

The distinction is easy to blur.

The Commonalty is not a friendlier state that arrives at the end of transition. It begins wherever society directly organizes a common power:

  • workers govern production rather than receiving commands from an owner or ministry;
  • residents govern local life through a commune;
  • connected communes coordinate a watershed, grid, railway, or health system;
  • public workers answer to the people using and sustaining the service;
  • knowledge moves out of administrative monopoly; and
  • people can inspect, correct, and replace delegated authority.

At first, these relations are not universal. They coexist with inherited administration, money, unequal development, military danger, political conflict, and habits formed by class society.

The Commonalty exists immediately without yet existing everywhere.

This correction matters. If it is postponed until every class relation has disappeared, then the transition has no positive communist form of its own. The state is left to govern society until some unexplained future moment when self-government appears.

The opposite error declares that any council or assembly is already the completed Commonalty. That ignores material dependence, international isolation, administrative separation, and the continuing need to defeat organized restoration.

The Commonalty begins as a real relation and develops toward universality.

Abolish, preserve, transform

The philosophical term sublation describes a movement that abolishes, preserves, and transforms at the same time. It fits the relation between state and Commonalty when used carefully.

Inherited power or function What must be abolished What must be preserved What must change
water system class access, secrecy, remote command pipes, maps, chemistry, maintenance workers and residents govern priorities and records
public health hierarchy of access, coercive neglect laboratories, clinics, epidemiology, trained care knowledge, prevention, and allocation enter common control
economic administration profitability, managerial sovereignty, hidden accounts material records, logistics, technical skill production begins from need and accountable plans
conflict resolution class law and inaccessible authority investigation, evidence, protection, settlement procedures become public, reviewable, and socially governed
armed defense standing power over the population capacity to defeat counterrevolution defense remains rooted in and subordinate to working-class organs

The table does not promise that every transformation will be smooth. An old institution may be too entangled with domination to reform. A necessary service may collapse unless workers preserve it during the break. Emergency defense may temporarily concentrate command.

The criterion is direction. Does the new form expand society’s ability to understand and perform the function? Does it narrow the separate power required to enforce class rule?

Withering is not waiting

Engels described the state as withering away once it no longer has a class to suppress. The phrase is often made passive, as if the state were a leaf that changes color when history’s weather becomes mild.

Withering is produced.

Common provision weakens the power of money. Socialization weakens private command. Shorter necessary labor gives people time to govern. Education spreads capacities previously held by specialists. Recall breaks the official’s security against the public. International revolution weakens the military pressures that preserve emergency forms. The transformation of care and household labor weakens dependencies that public democracy alone cannot reach.

At the same time, the Commonalty takes up more of society’s common work.

flowchart LR
    A["Class relations weaken"] --> B["Separate coercion has less work"]
    C["Common capacity grows"] --> D["More functions are directly reabsorbed"]
    B --> E["The state relation contracts"]
    D --> E
    E --> C

Text alternative: As class relations weaken, separate coercion has less work. As common capacity grows, more public functions are directly reabsorbed. Both movements contract the state relation and allow common capacity to grow further.

A government does not wither by calling every expansion temporary. It withers when its material functions either disappear or pass into forms people can exercise directly.

The sharpest test

The language of popular government can hide the same separation under a new emblem.

Ask who controls the map, the schedule, the budget, the archive, the weapon, the appointment, the right of appeal, and the channel through which bad news travels.

If workers and residents may vote but cannot reach any of these powers, they do not govern.

If delegates can be recalled but the office remains unintelligible, recall only changes the face.

If councils exist but a party bureau chooses their candidates and controls their resources, the class has not reabsorbed public power.

If specialists explain what is possible and the public chooses among real possibilities, expertise serves self-government. If specialists convert knowledge into a right to choose society’s ends, administration has become rule again.

The Commune did not solve every institutional problem. It gave the problem its direction.

The state returns to society when people recognize its useful powers as their own, acquire the means to exercise them, and strip away the class relation that made their own capacities stand above them.

The Commonalty is that return becoming a way of life.
