06Planning for Use and Need

Labour Accounting Without Labour Money

On one bank of a river, children travel forty minutes to school. On the other, the school is close enough to see. A bridge could pull the two sides together, but the bridge will take steel, cranes, engineers, concrete workers, drivers, cooks, safety inspectors, and a great piece of their lives.

Society needs to know how large that piece is. Labour accounting measures the time used in production. It does not require turning hours into money or pretending that every kind of work is identical.

Time is part of every plan

Marx writes that economy of time remains necessary under communal production. A society that spends fewer hours meeting one need gains time for other material, scientific, artistic, and personal activity.

That makes labour time a planning fact. A bridge account might include design, material production, transport, construction, inspection, training, maintenance, and labour already embodied in important inputs. The point is to see the social effort and organize it wisely. Nobody is buying the bridge from society.

The average is useful, but never innocent

Socially average labour time is the time normally required to perform an activity with the available methods and conditions.

If one workshop takes twelve hours to make a pump while similar workshops take eight, the difference deserves study. Its machinery may be older. Its material may arrive damaged. Its workers may have discovered a safer method. The number begins an investigation. It does not deliver a verdict.

An average can expose a bottleneck or hide a disability. It can spread a good invention or become a weapon for speed-up. Workers must help define the categories, inspect comparisons, and explain the conditions behind them.

Labour accounting serves freedom only when the producers govern the account.

One coordinate, not the whole map

An hour of surgery cannot be swapped mechanically with an hour of digging. The activities need different preparation, carry different risks, and produce different useful effects.

Communist planning can compare time within an activity and trace how activities depend on one another without declaring them equal in every quality. A plan may know that a hospital needs ten surgical hours, thirty nursing shifts, sterile equipment, and power. Collapsing all of that into one number would destroy information the hospital needs.

Time works alongside physical, ecological, and qualitative measures. It is one coordinate in a larger map.

Under capitalism, labour becomes socially validated through the exchange of products. Competition reduces different labours to abstract labour and presses producers toward the socially necessary time.

In communal production, the labour is recognized as social before it begins. Society can record that a pump usually takes eight hours without saying the pump possesses eight hours of value.

The difference lies in the relation, not the arithmetic. The account informs a plan governed by the producers. It does not validate private labour through exchange.

Labour certificates are a transitional question

In Marx’s lower phase of communism, a producer may receive a record of labour contributed after common deductions and draw a corresponding amount from the individual means of consumption.

A labour certificate differs from money if it cannot circulate, earn interest, hire labour, become capital, or pass from person to person. It is cancelled in use. It records a limited personal claim inside a common system.

This arrangement still carries bourgeois right because one equal measure is applied to unequal people. A person with dependants, illness, or greater needs can be disadvantaged even when the arithmetic is equal.

Common services and differentiated support must correct that inequality. As common provision and capacity grow, personal certificates lose their purpose. Labour-time accounts can remain useful socially after they stop regulating personal access.

Three forms that are often confused do different work:

Form What it does What makes it distinct
Money Buys goods, labour-power, and productive assets It circulates, accumulates, and can become capital
Personal labour certificate Records a limited claim on individual consumption It is cancelled in use and cannot circulate or hire labour
Social labour-time account Helps plan effort across production It belongs to the common plan and need not regulate anyone’s consumption

The difference is institutional, not a change of label.

A bridge teaches the distinction

Suppose two bridge designs meet the same safety and ecological standards. One takes more steel but fewer labour hours. The other uses local material, takes longer, and is easier to repair.

Labour accounting reveals one tradeoff. Physical accounting reveals another. Engineers explain maintenance, residents explain floods, and ecologists study the river. The population or its accountable bodies choose.

No honest planner says that the smaller labour number automatically wins. Saving time matters because human life matters, so speed cannot be allowed to sacrifice every other quality.

The clock must never become a whip

An hourly wage does not become communist because it receives a new name. Labour accounting also cannot make labour the only source of wealth. Nature supplies the material conditions of every use-value.

A person’s hours can never become a ranking of human worth. Children, older people, disabled people, carers, and anyone temporarily unable to work remain full members of society.

No certificate system is required everywhere or on a fixed timetable. Open questions include training time, intensity, dangerous work, care, irregular creative work, depreciation, international accounting, and how several measures can be combined without creating a technical caste.

Conclusion

Labour time is the piece of life production uses. A communist account makes that use visible so society can reduce necessary work, share burdens, and open time for human development. The bridge gives the children back part of every morning. That returned time belongs in the account too.
